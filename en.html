Summary

My previous experiences are based primarily on a strongly project-oriented study in the field of media. A special aspect of my study ist the use of digital media, both in single and collaborative projects. Noticable examples are the recording of a physically immersive audio play as well as the construction of a website-mockup. I am able to talk and write in several languages. Danish and English, I had the possibility to improve during a yearlong study in Denmark. I have maintained my language skills, since then. I am interested in new experiences, and, especially, in broadening my content managing, concept developping, and language skills.

Selected Projects

Jun. 2016 – Aug. 2016

Soundwalk Between inside and outside, Potsdam

The project is concerned with ascertaining the border between the inside of buildings and the urban outdoors. Individual audio points are arranged around the main building of FH Potsdam. They can be listened to through the application Radio Aporee. Tasks:

  • recording of the crossing between the urban outdoors and the building with binaural microphones

  • editing with REAPER

  • arranging recordings on Radio Aporee

Feb. 2016 – Mai 2016

Audio Play Darkroom, Potsdam

This physically immersive audio play is created in collaboration with the web developer Bjarke Walling. It analises the immersive potential of a dark room. Tasks:

  • voice and field recordings with binaural and condenser microphones

  • editing with REAPER

  • arranging of a physically immersive installation in the video laboratory of FH Potsdam

Jan. 2015 – Apr. 2015

WordPress-Mockup Tweet Chaos Collection, Potsdam

This project examines, how randomly collected tweets and their arrangement as blog posts result in a meaningful collection. Meaningful means in this case, that the tweet-collections refer to an online identity. Tasks:

  • collecting of tweets

  • sorting them in categories

  • designing pictures and diagrams with Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator

  • arranging collected tweets on a blog

Sep. 2013

Translation Work for Amazonkey (Now Zonky Fruits), Berlin

Zonky Fruits is a fair-trade company. Through the cultivation and sale of fruits from the Amazon it is contributing to the strengthening of the local economy and the maintenance of the rainforest. Tasks:

  • translating of texts from English to German

Nov. 2012 – Mrz. 2013

Enhanced E-Book Concept Intercultural Education, Potsdam

The concept is created in relation to the seminar "`Crossmedia Format Development"'. It is the concept of an enhanced e-book with teaching material for language and culture teachers of pupils between the age of thirteen and sixteen. Its goal is to make those teachers familiar with the use of pluralistic approaches while teaching a foureign language. Tasks:

  • conception of an enhanced e-book including details about the design, content, structure, and the background of the format

During this internship, I support the project "`Framework of of references for pluralistic approachesto languanges and cultures"'. Tasks:

  • observation of lessons at the International Profile School in Copenhagen (French, German and Danish)

  • interview with the German teacher
